Who We Are

Your Trusted Local
Food Plug

La Spag is a popular Lagos-based food spot specializing in delicious,loaded stir-fry-spaghetti and other fast-casuals meals. Operating mainly on the Lagos Mainland (with spots like Isolo and Amuwo-Odofin), it's known as a go-to for fresh pasta bowls, street-food style daily plates, and hearty combos featuring grilled chicken, turkey, plantain(dodo), and rich, flavourful sauces that go way beyond basic spaghetti.

Whether it's our signature sausage-infused spag,turkey specials, or affordable plates that hit the spot, La Spag has become a vibe for foodies craving something tasty, quick, and far from ordinary
